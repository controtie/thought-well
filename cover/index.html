Stack Overflow + Bitcoin.

Stack Overflow functions largely as a Q&A archive. As a result, it inherits some challenges of query based systems. Stack Overflow in its truest form should function as a marketplace.

Hypothesis: An economy based on sufficiently small transactions alters the behavior of its members, specifically in their attachment to money. An on-demand Q&A marketplace with enough volume can serve the needs of both question askers and answerers.
